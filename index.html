About Survey Mania

Survey Mania is an online platform that allows users to create, distribute, and analyze surveys with ease. It offers a user-friendly interface for both survey creators and respondents. The key feature of Survey Mania is its ability to generate visual representations of survey data, making it easier for users to gain insights from the responses.
